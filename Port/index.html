About

Quantity Surveyor, Cost Estimator, and engineer by training

Quantity Surveyor and Cost Estimator with a Mechanical Engineering background and hands-on experience across residential and industrial construction. I specialise in residential quantity take-offs, BOQ preparation, and cost estimation support for Australian builders, using COSTX as my primary take-off software alongside Bluebeam and PlanSwift.

My engineering training shows up in how I work — methodical drawing interpretation, close attention to measurement accuracy, and estimates that hold up under scrutiny at tender stage. I've supported estimating teams through bidding, subcontractor coordination, and client-facing project documentation, and I bring the same discipline to every take-off, whether it's a single dwelling or a multi-unit residential development.
